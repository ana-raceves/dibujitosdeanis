About me

This is Ana, I born in Guadalajara, Mexico but I've been living in different parts of Mexico trying to discover new things to do, I consider myself a curious person who always is wondering why happen everything. I love nature and food as you can see drawn in my illustrations, I also enjoy spend time alone having crative moments. I am brand new digital illustrator; I've been drawing since I have memory using different tecniques like acrilic, water colours, oil pastels, etc. But a few months ago, I've decided to start using tecnology yo create my art job. I can find inspiration in a lot of things but mostly in daily life, mexican food, flowers and nature, so here you are the results.

I hope you enjoy and like my art work!
